Features

Built for speed, privacy, and zero friction. Every feature serves one purpose: getting your words onto the screen.

Works in Every App

ChatGPT, Slack, Notion, VS Code, browsers, email — wherever you can type, Hikari Yaps can paste. No integrations needed.

100% Offline & Private

Your voice never leaves your Mac. All AI runs locally on the Neural Engine and GPU. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking.

Smart Cleanup

A local AI model strips "ums" and "uhs", fixes grammar, and polishes your text — all in under a second. Choose from Message, Professional, Concise, or Code tone.

Vocabulary & Snippets

Teach Hikari Yaps your jargon, names, and custom shortcuts. Say "my email" and your full address appears. Say "sig" and your signature drops in.

Command Mode

Say "new line" or "new paragraph" to insert breaks. The app understands spoken formatting commands and structures your text automatically.

History & Export

Every transcription is saved locally with word counts and session stats. Search, copy, or export your history as text or JSON anytime.

How It Works

Three Steps. Zero Friction.

01

Hold Your Hotkey

Default is Ctrl. Hold it down while you speak. A tiny red dot and waveform bars appear at the bottom of your screen so you know it's listening.

02

Talk Naturally

Speak at your normal pace. The Neural Engine processes your voice in real time. Say "new line" for line breaks, "new paragraph" for paragraphs.

Hey team, so regarding the
Q3 roadmap, I think we should
prioritize the API refactor...
03

Release & Watch It Type

Let go of the key. The text appears instantly in whatever field you were focused on — ChatGPT, Slack, a code editor, anywhere. A quick gold checkmark confirms it's done.
